About Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital

Our Mission

At Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital, our mission is to provide exceptional emergency medical care to our community. We are committed to treating every patient with dignity, respect, and compassion while delivering the highest standard of medical excellence.

Emergency Department

Our Emergency Department is a Level I Trauma Center, equipped to handle the most critical cases. We operate 24/7 with a team of board-certified emergency physicians, trauma surgeons, and specialized nursing staff.

Triage System

Our digital triage system is designed to:

  • Reduce patient wait times through efficient prioritization
  • Provide real-time updates on estimated wait times
  • Enable seamless communication between patients and staff
  • Ensure critical cases receive immediate attention
